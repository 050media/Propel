I'll have the pleasure to be giving a presentation about Propel during next Forum PHP in Paris, on November 9th. Yep, that's in France, in only two weeks from now, and the presentation will be in French. 

This conference is also a unique occasion to listen to some of the most famous lecturers in the PHP world, including Rasmus Lerdorf, Zeev Suraski, Jonathan Wage, and Michael Widenius aka Monty. 

If you want to discover Propel, meet me and other Propel developers in real life, make sure to grab a ticket for the event.
