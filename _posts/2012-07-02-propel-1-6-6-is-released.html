--- layout: post title: Propel 1.6.6 is Released published: true ---

Propel 1.6.6 is now available, and it's the third bug fixes only release.

The release is available on GitHub under the 1.6.6 tag on GitHub, as PEAR package (runtime & generator), as archives (ZIP and TAR), and also available through Composer: propel/propel1.

As usual, the API documentation is at: http://api.propelorm.org/, and here is the CHANGELOG. Thanks to all contributors.
