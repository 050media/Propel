--- layout: post title: Propel 1.4.2 Is Out published: true ---

The Propel 1.4 branch has a new minor release.

Update

  • Subversion tag

    > svn checkout http://svn.propelorm.org/tags/1.4.2

  • PEAR package

    > sudo pear upgrade phpdb/propel-generator
    > sudo pear upgrade phpdb/propel-runtime

Changelog

This is a bugfix release, backwards compatible with the 1.4 branch. It corrects a few bugs found in the past three months. The changelog is available at http://www.propelorm.org/wiki/Documentation/1.4/CHANGELOG.
